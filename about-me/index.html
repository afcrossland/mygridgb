About Me

Prof Andrew Crossland CEng

My mission is to help enable global decarbonisation, making energy more affordable and empowering people to take ownership.

I have spent my career working at the intersection of engineering, data, and energy policy. From a PhD at Durham University through international roles in renewable energy, battery storage, and microgrid development — spanning Africa, the Pacific Islands, New Zealand, and the UK — I have been driven by a single goal: making clean energy work in the real world.

MyGridGB grew out of that work. It started as a personal project to track the decarbonisation of British electricity in real time, and has grown into one of the UK's most-cited independent energy data resources.
