Clean energy investment fell 3% in 2017, and expect another fall in 2018, says a worried IEA

July 17, 2018 By Dr Andrew Crossland

World Energy Investment 2018: All energy $1.8 trillion. Renewables down 7% to $298bn. Energy efficiency up 3% to $236 bn. = $534bn incl. big hydro.  Fossil fuels $790bn. Electricity $750bn.

Fatih Birol, IEA Executive Director: Worrying. While we would need this investment to go up rapidly, it is disappointing to find that it might be falling this year.”
