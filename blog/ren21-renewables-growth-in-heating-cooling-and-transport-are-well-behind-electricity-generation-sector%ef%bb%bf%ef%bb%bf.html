REN21: Renewables growth in heating, cooling, and transport are well behind the electricity generation sector﻿﻿

4 Jun 2018 · Jeremy Leggett
This is a guest post by Jeremy Leggett. Views are the author's own and do not necessarily represent the opinions or positions of MyGridGB or Dr Andrew Crossland.

Rana Adib, executive secretary of REN21: warns of “complacency” ….“we are coasting along as if we had all the time in the world. Sadly, we don’t.”

The report contains all the statistics we in the renewables sector have come to rely on each year, beautifully presented as ever. But the comparison between power and the other sectors is the main point that REN21 draws out from all the detail, and it is a good one. We cannot expect electricity-as-a-fuel to be a cure-all.
