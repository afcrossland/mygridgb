UK’s Green New Deal Group calls for a “green jobs in every constituency” investment push

September 10, 2018 By Dr Andrew Crossland

Ten years after the Lehman collapse, the group says that had its call at that time been heeded, the rise of populism could have been mitigated.

Business Green reports that “the group is calling on political parties and local campaigners to get behind the idea ofa dedicated green stimulus programme, which it says would inject a “sense of hope” in society.”
