Trump orders emergency federal action to stem coal and nuclear plant shutdowns

June 2, 2018 By Dr Andrew Crossland

Proposals in a leaked memo include forcing utilities to buy electricity from coal and nuclear operators for 2 years.

This would be an unprecedented intervention by the government in “free” energy markets – active subversion of renewables, and gas for that matter. Trump’s authoritarian tendencies are increasingly becoming an overt ally of the energy incumbency in its desperate rearguard action.
