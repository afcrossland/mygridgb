“Why Tech Worker Dissent Is Going Viral”: Wired headline – protest is “tearing through” tech giants

June 29, 2018 By Dr Andrew Crossland

“Within a few days in late June, employees from Microsoft, Amazon, and Salesforce publicized petitions urging their CEOs to cancel or rethink lucrative contracts with US Customs and Border Protection, Immigration and Customs Enforcement, and local police departments.”

Wired’s investigation into why includes this quote by a Google employee: “People who signed up to be tech heroes don’t want to be implicated in human rights abuses.”
