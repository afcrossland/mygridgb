“Our climate plans are in pieces as killer summer shreds records”: CNN review of heatwaves around world

5 Aug 2018 · Jeremy Leggett
This is a guest post by Jeremy Leggett. Views are the author's own and do not necessarily represent the opinions or positions of MyGridGB or Dr Andrew Crossland.

The article reviews extreme events in the US and globally. The conclusion is that because of the picture emerging, “more and more Americans are starting to accept climate change is happening, despite Trump’s pledge to pull the US from the Paris Agreement. American acceptance of climate change returned to an all-time high of 71% in October last year after sliding significantly from around a decade ago, according to the Yale Program on Climate Change Communication.

The world’s climate plans are not in pieces. Or those of non-federal America. The fight is still on.
