“World’s Biggest AI Startup Raises $1.2 Billion in Mere Months”: Bloomberg on Sense Time Group

May 31, 2018 By Dr Andrew Crossland

China “is ramping up spending on surveillance as it cracks down hard on restive parts of the country, including Xinjiang.” Sense Time Group is a front runner in that. As Bloomberg puts it: “SenseTime specializes in systems that analyze faces and images on an enormous scale and works with policing bodies across China.”

Investors include: Alibaba Group Holding Ltd, Singapore’s state investment firm, Fidelity International and Silver Lake Partners, Tiger Capital, Qualcomm Ventures and Hopu Capital.
