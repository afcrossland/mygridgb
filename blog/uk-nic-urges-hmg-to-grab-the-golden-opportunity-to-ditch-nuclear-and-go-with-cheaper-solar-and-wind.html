UK NIC urges HMG to grab the ‘golden opportunity’ to ditch nuclear and go with cheaper solar and wind

July 10, 2018 By Dr Andrew Crossland

This in the UK’s first ever National Infrastructure Assessment: at least half UK power should be renewable by 2030, & can be at no extra cost.

So now they have all key relevant advisors/regulators urging them to go green: the Climate Change Committee, Ofgem, and now the National Infrastructure Committee. Were they not so preoccupied with Brexit, might they have turned by now?
